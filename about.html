About

Phil Fifield, Founder

I didn't start in consulting. I started in operations. Running restaurants, managing multi-unit teams, building systems from the ground up because there wasn't a playbook to follow.

As Director of Operations for a multi-location restaurant group, I learned what actually works when you're responsible for the outcome. Not theories. Not frameworks. The real work of making a business run consistently, every day, across every location.

I built Bluegrass Advisory Group to bring that operator's perspective to businesses that have outgrown their current systems. Whether you're managing 3 locations or 300 employees, the problem is the same: you need infrastructure that scales as fast as you do.

We build dashboards, SOPs, automated reporting, and AI-powered systems. But the real deliverable is clarity. Knowing exactly where your business stands, every morning, without asking anyone.

Based in Lexington, Kentucky

What we believe

These aren't slogans. They're the principles that shape every engagement.

Discovery before diagnosis

We learn your operation before we prescribe anything. Every business is different, and cookie-cutter solutions don't survive contact with reality.

Every recommendation is backed by what the numbers actually say. We measure first, then act. No gut feelings dressed up as strategy.

Build, don't just advise

We deliver working systems, not slide decks. Dashboards your team logs into. SOPs your managers reference. Tools that get used every day.

Transfer ownership

Your team runs it when we leave. We build for independence, not dependency. The goal is to make ourselves unnecessary.
